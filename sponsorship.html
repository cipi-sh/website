Support open source

Keep Cipi free and growing.

Cipi is MIT-licensed and always free. Sponsoring covers server costs, development time, and helps prioritise new features. Every contribution — no matter the size — makes a real difference.

Why your support matters

Cipi has no VC funding, no paid tier, and no hidden costs. It's maintained by a solo developer in spare time.

  • Your name or company link listed in the official Cipi Sponsorship area
  • Faster bug fixes and feature prioritisation for reported issues
  • Direct contribution to keeping the tool free for thousands of developers
  • Monthly sponsors keep the lights on: CI/CD pipelines, domains, and test VPS instances
